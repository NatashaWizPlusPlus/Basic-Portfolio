About Me

I was born and raised in The Netherlands - Europe and moved to the United States in 2008. Getting cultured is my number one passion, knowing people, places, religions, phrases and foods of different ethnicities. I have worked in different countries and still always long for exploring and deep diving into the possibilities this world has to offer.
I studied Computer Science with an emphasis in Software Engineering and am a driver of innovative technology. When I am not at my full-time job, I am a consultant and various start-ups. I have joined the Coding Bootcamp to practice fluency using front-end languages and position myself back in the "everyday coding" seat.
In my free time I am hypnotized by music production, explore hiking trails with my dog and always in search for bakeries with the best custard pastries.
